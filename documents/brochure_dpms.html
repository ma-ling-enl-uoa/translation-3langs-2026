About the Programme

A unique trilingual MA: three Departments, three languages, one academic programme of excellence. Translation across Greek, English and Russian is not a transfer of words. It is intercultural, historical and human practice, and it is taught here as such.

Trilingual translation is not an exchange of words; it is the construction of bridges between cultures that machines cannot architect.

Admission

No written entrance examination. Portfolio evaluation (70%) and oral interview (30%).

Proficiency certificates required for all three working languages: English, Greek and Russian, at C1 level.

Eligible Applicants
◆Graduates in English Language and Literature, Greek Philology, Russian Language and Slavic Studies, and cognate fields.
◆Graduates covering core fields of Linguistics and Translation Studies.
◆Holders of equivalent degrees from recognised foreign institutions.
